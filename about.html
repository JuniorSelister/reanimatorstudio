about reanimator studio

we are reanimator

Reanimator Studio is an independent collective founded by Marginal2D and Conrado de Paula, a designer, illustrator and animation director from Porto Alegre, Brazil.

Services include graphics and illustrations for 2D animation films, institutional and corporate videos, game design, art education, modelling, sculpture and fashion design.
