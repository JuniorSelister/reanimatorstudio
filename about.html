who is reanimator?

Conrado de Paula is a designer, illustrator, comic book artist, screenwriter, video editor and animation director from Porto Alegre, Brazil. Born in 1987, his works include every type of graphic material to 2D animation films, as well as institutional and commercial films for various market niches. In addition to projects in art education, Conrado also works with modelling, sculpture and fashion design.
